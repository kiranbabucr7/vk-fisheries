About VK Fisheries

VK Fisheries works closely with farmers and landowners who want to earn extra income through fish farming.

If you have land and water, we help you set up the pond, choose the right fish, and guide you on daily care and maintenance.

Our team regularly supports you, and when the fish are fully grown, we come directly to your land and collect the harvest.

This means less risk, clear guidance, and peaceful farming with professional support from start to finish.
